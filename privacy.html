Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Christ Cornerstone Ministries LLC (doing business as Get Sales Tech and NeverMiss AI) collects, uses, and protects information from visitors, customers, and users of our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

This may include your name, business name, phone number, email address, and other contact details. We may also collect automated data such as your IP address, browser type, and site usage statistics through analytics tools and cookies.

We use the information we collect to:

3. Text Messaging (SMS) Policy

By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive SMS messages from Christ Cornerstone Ministries LLC related to appointment confirmations, reminders, or promotional offers. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for assistance at any time.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with trusted service providers who assist in delivering our services (e.g., scheduling, payment processing, SMS delivery) under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also opt out of promotional communications at any time by replying STOP to SMS messages or contacting us directly.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be posted on this page with an updated “Effective Date.”
